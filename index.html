My Services As A...

Shopify Expert

When passion, expertise, and attention to detail are combined, the result is a beautiful and successful online store.

Theme Development

Specialized in custom Shopify theme development, whether working with established stores to redesign their entire store without any design limitations, or building unique storefront functionality tailored to the client's specific needs. My expertise in coding and understanding of the Shopify platform allows me to bring any vision to life, delivering a polished and fully-functional online store, translating unique business requirements into custom solutions on Shopify.

Store Optimization

Strong experience in optimizing Shopify stores for performance, technical SEO, and conversion rate optimization to increase sales and improve the customer experience. Implementing performance optimization techniques, utilizing tools such as Google Analytics and Hotjar to identify areas for improvement and track the success of optimization efforts, testing and optimizing elements such as website layout, product pages, and checkout flows to increase conversions and improve the user experience including A/B testing with Google Optimize. Collaborating with clients to understand their business goals and implementing strategies to achieve them.

Migration Services

Helping businesses migrate from their existing e-commerce platforms to Shopify, ensuring a seamless transition and preserving important data.

Consultation, Training and Support

Comprehensive training and support to ensure that store owners have the knowledge and tools necessary to effectively manage their online store. From basic navigation and management to advanced customization and optimization, I provide personalized, step-by-step guidance to empower store owners to take control of their online presence. As a merchant, the true beauty of entrepreneurship lies in the ability to bring your vision to life, to turn your passion into a thriving business. The technicalities of running that business should not be a burden, but rather a means to an end. By focusing on the bigger picture, on the passion and drive that led you to start your business in the first place, you can leave the technicalities to experts like Shopify Developers, and truly soar as an entrepreneur. The key to success is to stay true to your vision and let the support team worry about the technical side of things.

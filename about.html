Oh, hello there!

My name is Valentin Lako. As an Senior Shopify Developer, I bring a unique blend of technical expertise and creative vision to the table. With over 6 years of experience in the industry, I've had the privilege of working with many clients, ranging from startups to 7-8 figure businesses, helping them to establish and grow their online presence.

My love for technology began at a very young age and has only grown stronger over time. I have always been fascinated by the endless possibilities that coding has to offer and how it can be used to create beautiful and functional online storefronts.

I believe that entrepreneurship is a noble pursuit, and I am passionate about helping merchants succeed in the digital landscape. I understand that the technical aspects of building and maintaining an online store can be overwhelming, and that's where I come in. I aim to take on the technical burden so that merchants can focus on what they do best: running their business.

In my free time, I enjoy playing table tennis and reading books. I find that the fast-paced nature of table tennis helps to clear my mind and allows me to come back to work feeling refreshed and energized. I also enjoy immersing myself in a good book, whether it's a novel, non-fiction, self-development or a technical book, as it helps me to relax and expand my knowledge. Both table tennis and reading are great ways for me to unwind and recharge.
